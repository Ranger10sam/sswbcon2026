Welcome to SSWBCON 2026

Advancing Spine Surgery in Bengal

The Spine Society of West Bengal brings together eminent spine surgeons from across India and the world. SSWBCON 2026 continues our tradition of excellence in spine education, research, and surgical innovation.

Experience world-class scientific sessions alongside the rich cultural heritage of Kolkata—the City of Joy.
