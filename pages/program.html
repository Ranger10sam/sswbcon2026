CME Credit Days

Day 1

Pre-Conference Workshop Day

Saturday, June 20, 2026

08:00 - 13:00 Cadaver Lab

Cervical Spine Dissection Workshop

Hands-on cadaveric training for anterior and posterior cervical approaches. Limited to 20 participants.

08:00 - 13:00 Simulation Center

MIS Lumbar Decompression

Minimally invasive surgery techniques on simulation models. Tubular retractor and endoscopic approaches.

14:00 - 17:00 Hall A

Pedicle Screw Instrumentation

Freehand and navigation-assisted pedicle screw placement techniques on synthetic spine models.

14:00 - 17:00 Hall B

Spinal Injections Workshop

Fluoroscopy-guided diagnostic and therapeutic spinal injections for pain management specialists.

Day 2

Main Conference - Degenerative Spine

Sunday, June 21, 2026

09:00 - 10:00 Main Hall

Inauguration & Keynote Address

"Evolution of Spine Surgery in India - Past, Present & Future" by Guest of Honor

10:30 - 12:30 Main Hall

Lumbar Disc Herniation

Panel discussion on microsurgery vs endoscopy, recurrence prevention, and when to fuse.

14:00 - 16:00 Hall A

Cervical Myelopathy Management

Anterior vs posterior approaches, motion preservation options, and complex cases.

16:30 - 18:00 Main Hall

Live Surgery Demonstration

HD-streamed live surgery with expert commentary from the operating suite.

19:30 onwards Cultural Program

Gala Dinner & Bengali Cultural Evening

Experience authentic Bengali hospitality with traditional music, dance, and cuisine at a heritage venue in Kolkata.

Day 3

Deformity & Trauma

Monday, June 22, 2026

09:00 - 11:00 Main Hall

Adult Spinal Deformity

Sagittal balance assessment, surgical planning, and complication management.

09:00 - 11:00 Hall A

Spine Trauma & Fractures

TLICS scoring, timing of surgery, and construct selection for thoracolumbar trauma.

11:30 - 13:00 Main Hall

Adolescent Idiopathic Scoliosis

Fusion strategies, growing rod technology, and vertebral body tethering.

14:00 - 15:30 Main Hall

Free Paper Presentations

Selected oral presentations from submitted abstracts. Awards to be announced.

16:00 - 17:00 Main Hall

Valedictory Session & Awards

Best paper awards, acknowledgments, and announcement of SSWBCON 2027 venue. CME certificates distribution.
