Travel Tips for Kolkata

By Air

Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport (CCU) is 17 km from the venue. Prepaid taxis available.

By Train

Howrah Junction and Sealdah are the main stations. Both are well-connected by metro and taxis.

Accommodation

Partner hotels offer special rates. Book through our registration portal for exclusive discounts.

Weather

March in Kolkata is pleasant (20-32°C). Light cotton clothing recommended.
